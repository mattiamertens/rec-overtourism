Good practices to reduce the impact
1.
Get off the beaten path and avoid tourist traps, don’t explore the same bucket list destination as everyone
2.
Don’t plan an overpacked itinerary, try to slow down and enjoy less location for longer
3.
Choose the most sustainable transportation mean, like trains or buses
4.
Respect the locals and their communities. Residents are what make the location worthy
Why
overtourism
Access to various locations in the last two years has been limited, and not only for health reasons. There are locations that had to close due to overtourism, fuelled by TV-shows, movies and Instagram posts, and not sustainable practices which eventually exceeded the foreseen tourism carrying capacity. Now these places need a certain amount of recovery time, without any tourists, and before reopening different policies are being enforced to limit the amount of visitors and to shelter the ecosystem.
